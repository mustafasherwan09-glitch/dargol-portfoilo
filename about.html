Our Story

About Dargol Logistics

Born in the heart of UAE–Iraq trade, built on trust, speed, and an uncompromising commitment to your cargo's safety.

Our Mission

Connecting Dubai to Erbil, One Shipment at a Time

Dargol Logistics was established with a singular vision: to create the most reliable, fastest, and most affordable freight corridor between Dubai (UAE) and Erbil (Kurdistan Region, Iraq).

We understand the unique challenges of UAE–Iraq trade — complex customs, cross-border regulations, and the critical importance of timely delivery. Our deep expertise in both markets allows us to navigate these challenges seamlessly, offering our clients unparalleled peace of mind.

Our Journey

Company Timeline

From a small freight office in Dubai to the leading UAE–Iraq logistics specialist.

✦
2017
Dargol Logistics Founded in Dubai
The company was established in Dubai with a focus on land freight between UAE and Iraq, serving early customers with a single truck route to Erbil.
✦
2018
Erbil Office Opens on Gazna Road
To better serve our Iraqi customers, we opened our Erbil branch on Gazna Road — the logistics hub of Kurdistan. Same-day delivery for local Erbil clients launched.
✦
2020
Air Freight Service Launched
We introduced our express air freight service, cutting Dubai-Erbil delivery time from 5–7 days to as fast as 24–48 hours. Partnership with leading air cargo operators established.
✦
2021
Shein Package Delivery Program Starts
Recognizing the explosive growth of Shein orders in Kurdistan, we launched our dedicated Shein consolidation service. Over 5,000 packages delivered in the first year.
✦
2023
Sea Freight & Container Shipping Added
Full container (FCL) and groupage (LCL) sea freight services launched for larger volumes. Partnerships with Jebel Ali port operators enable global shipping capabilities.
⭐
2024–2026
Digital Transformation & App Launch
Real-time tracking portal launched. Development of the Dargol mobile app in progress. 15,000+ shipments delivered. Recognized as the #1 Dubai–Erbil freight specialist.
