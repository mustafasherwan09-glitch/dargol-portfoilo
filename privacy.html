Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 1, 2026 — How Dargol Logistics protects your data and manages cargo liability.

Table of Contents

1. Data Collection 2. How We Use Your Data 3. Data Sharing 4. Cargo Liability 5. Data Security 6. Cookies 7. Terms of Service 8. Contact Us

Dargol Logistics ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting the personal information of our clients, customers, and website visitors. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our services or visit our website. Please read this policy carefully. If you disagree with its terms, please discontinue use of our services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us when you use our services:

  • Personal Identification: Full name, email address, phone number (WhatsApp), and physical address for delivery purposes.
  • Shipment Information: Package contents, weight, dimensions, declared value, origin and destination addresses, and Bill of Lading details.
  • Financial Information: Payment records (we do not store full card details; payments are processed through secure third-party providers).
  • Communication Records: WhatsApp messages, emails, and contact form submissions for customer support purposes.
  • Website Usage Data: IP address, browser type, pages visited, and time spent on pages (via analytics tools).

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Process and manage your shipment bookings and deliveries
  • Provide real-time shipment tracking updates
  • Handle customs documentation and clearance on your behalf
  • Send order confirmations, tracking notifications, and delivery alerts via WhatsApp, SMS, and email
  • Respond to customer service inquiries and resolve disputes
  • Comply with UAE and Iraq customs and legal requirements
  • Improve our services, website, and mobile application
  • Send promotional offers and logistics news (with your consent)

3. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell your personal data. We may share your information with:

  • Customs Authorities: UAE Federal Authority for Identity & Citizenship, Iraqi General Customs Authority — as legally required for cargo clearance.
  • Logistics Partners: Airlines, trucking companies, and port operators involved in the physical transport of your goods (limited to what is necessary for delivery).
  • Insurance Providers: For cargo insurance claims processing.
  • Legal Compliance: When required by law, court order, or regulatory authority in UAE or Iraq.

All third parties are contractually obligated to protect your data to the same standard we apply.

4. Cargo Liability & Insurance

Dargol Logistics cargo liability terms:

  • Standard Coverage: All shipments are covered for loss or damage up to the declared value, subject to our Standard Cargo Insurance.
  • Prohibited Items: Dargol Logistics does not accept illegal goods, currency, weapons, or items banned under UAE or Iraqi law. Discovery of such items will result in immediate reporting to authorities and forfeiture.
  • Declared Value: Clients are responsible for accurately declaring the value of their shipment. Underdeclaration may affect insurance claims.
  • Customs Compliance: Dargol Logistics acts as a customs broker on your behalf. Clients are ultimately responsible for ensuring goods are legally importable under Iraqi law.
  • Damage Claims: All damage claims must be reported within 7 days of delivery with photographic evidence.
  • Force Majeure: Dargol Logistics is not liable for delays caused by customs inspections, border closures, weather, war, or other events beyond our control.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information including:

  • SSL/TLS encryption for all data transmitted through our website
  • Secure, access-controlled servers with regular security audits
  • Staff training on data protection and privacy obligations
  • Data retention policies — personal data is deleted after 5 years unless legally required to be kept longer

Despite our best efforts, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. If you believe your data has been compromised, please contact us immediately.

6. Cookies & Tracking

Our website uses cookies to enhance your browsing experience. Types of cookies we use:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for the website to function correctly (e.g., session management, language preferences).
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with our website (aggregated, non-personal data).
  • Marketing Cookies: Used to show relevant advertisements (optional; can be disabled).

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling essential cookies may affect website functionality.

7. Terms of Service

By using Dargol Logistics services, you agree to:

  • Provide accurate and complete information for all shipment bookings
  • Pay all applicable freight charges, customs duties, and taxes
  • Not ship prohibited or illegal items as defined under UAE and Iraqi law
  • Comply with all relevant import/export regulations of the UAE and Republic of Iraq
  • Not hold Dargol Logistics liable for delays or losses caused by customs authorities or force majeure events
  • Allow Dargol Logistics to open and inspect any package if required by customs authorities

These terms are governed by the laws of the United Arab Emirates. Any disputes shall be subject to the jurisdiction of UAE courts or, by mutual agreement, arbitration.
